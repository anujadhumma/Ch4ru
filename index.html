Help & Policies

  • Return Policy

    • You can return any saree within 7 days of receiving it.

    Please note: Returns are not accepted for any product damaged by the customer or for requests due to color changes.

    To initiate a return, contact us with your order details, and our team will assist you.

  • How to Buy & Payment

    • To place an order, reach out to us on any platform listed on our Contact page.

    When contacting us, please provide the following details:

    • Name of the saree (as displayed on our website)
    • Color of the saree

    Payment can be made via Google Pay (GPay) to the number listed on our Contact page.

    Once payment is confirmed, your order will be processed, and you’ll receive your saree within 5-10 days.

  • Help & Frequently Asked Questions

    • For additional questions, details on color options, or further information on any saree, feel free to contact us.

    Our team is here to help you with any queries about our sarees, including fabric, size, or special features.

  • Quality Assurance

    • We guarantee that all sarees are authentic and of high quality.

    If the saree does not meet your expectations, you are welcome to return it (subject to our return policy).
